## **1. Core Idea**

**SDEOT** proposes that **dark energy originates from quantum fluctuations at the boundaries of universes** rather than being directly transferred from one universe to another.

* High-entropy (dying) universes approach a state of maximum disorder.
* Near this state, interactions at the **quantum boundary** of the universe produce energy fluctuations.
* These fluctuations manifest as **dark energy** in neighboring low-entropy (young) universes, driving their accelerated expansion.
* Information from the dying universe is encoded in these fluctuations, preserving causal continuity across the multiverse.

> In essence: **Dark energy is generated indirectly** as a natural byproduct of high-entropy universes nearing collapse, powering the expansion of young universes.
